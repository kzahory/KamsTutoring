About Me

As a nurse and biology graduate from the University of Virginia, I bring a strong foundation in both science and clinical care. I hold a BA in Biology and a Master of Science in Nursing from UVA, with advanced coursework in Pharmacology, Pathophysiology, and Critical Care. I tutor most biology, chemistry, and nursing courses, including subjects like Introductory and Organic Chemistry, Introductory Biology, Genetics, Ecology & Evolution, Med-Surg, and more. My approach is clear, supportive, and designed to help students succeed in the most challenging STEM and pre-health courses.
